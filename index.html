Baby Gambling: Organizational Structure and Responsibility Guide

Company Overview

Baby Gambling specializes in providing high-quality, innovative products for newborns and their parents. Our mission is to make the early days of parenthood easier and more enjoyable through thoughtfully designed, safe, and practical baby essentials.

Roles and Responsibilities

CEO (Muhammad Shahas)

  • Set overall company strategy and vision
  • Oversee financial health of the company
  • Final decision maker on major company initiatives
  • Liaison with investors and key stakeholders
  • Oversee and approve budgets
  • Ensure alignment across all departments

CTO & CMO (Praveen)

  • Develop and implement technology strategy
  • Oversee all marketing initiatives
  • Lead digital transformation efforts
  • Approve marketing campaigns and branding decisions
  • Oversee customer data management and analysis
  • Manage technology infrastructure and systems

COO (Thahsy)

  • Manage day-to-day operations of the company
  • Oversee supply chain and logistics
  • Implement and maintain quality control processes
  • Manage inventory and warehousing
  • Optimize operational processes for efficiency
  • Oversee customer service operations

Accountant (Binsi)

  • Manage company finances and bookkeeping
  • Prepare financial reports and analysis
  • Handle payroll and employee benefits
  • Ensure compliance with financial regulations
  • Assist in budget preparation and monitoring
  • Manage accounts payable and receivable

Product Designer (Yadu)

  • Design new products for Baby Gambling
  • Conduct market research for product development
  • Create product prototypes and iterate designs
  • Collaborate with marketing on product presentations
  • Ensure products meet safety and quality standards
  • Provide input on manufacturing processes

Graphic Designer (Musamil)

  • Create visual designs for marketing materials
  • Develop social media graphics and content
  • Create packaging designs for products
  • Ensure brand consistency across all materials
  • Collaborate with marketing on campaign visuals
  • Design user interface elements for digital platforms

Customer Relationship Manager (Masna Tasni)

  • Manage customer inquiries and complaints
  • Develop and implement customer service policies
  • Analyze customer feedback and report insights
  • Train and oversee customer service team
  • Implement CRM systems and processes
  • Collaborate with marketing on customer retention strategies

Digital Marketing Specialist (Anusha)

  • Develop and execute digital marketing strategies
  • Manage social media accounts and campaigns
  • Conduct SEO and SEM activities
  • Analyze marketing data and adjust strategies accordingly
  • Collaborate with graphic designer on marketing materials
  • Manage email marketing campaigns
  • Manage website updates and maintenance

Key Workflows

Product Development

Yadu leads, consults with Musamil for packaging, Thahsy for manufacturing, Praveen and Muhammad approve, Anusha provides market insights.

Marketing Campaigns

Anusha develops strategies, consults with Musamil for visuals, Praveen approves, Masna provides customer insights.

Financial Planning

Binsi prepares, consults with Thahsy, Muhammad approves, all department heads provide input.

Customer Service

Masna manages, escalates to Thahsy, reports to Praveen and Muhammad, collaborates with Yadu on product inquiries.

Website Updates

Anusha manages updates, consults with Yadu for product content, Musamil for visual elements, Praveen approves major changes.

Communication Guidelines

  • Weekly team meetings for cross-departmental alignment
  • Daily stand-ups within departments for quick updates
  • Use of project management tools for task tracking and collaboration
  • Monthly one-on-one meetings between team members and their direct supervisors
  • Quarterly all-hands meetings for company-wide updates and strategy discussions
  • Open-door policy encouraged for addressing concerns or sharing ideas

Decision-Making Processes

  • Strategic decisions: Led by Muhammad with input from Praveen and Thahsy
  • Marketing decisions: Led by Praveen with input from Anusha and Musamil
  • Operational decisions: Led by Thahsy with input from relevant team members
  • Financial decisions: Led by Muhammad with input from Binsi
  • Product decisions: Led by Yadu with approval from Muhammad and input from Praveen

Performance Management

  • Monthly performance check-ins with direct supervisors
  • Quarterly goal-setting and review sessions
  • Annual comprehensive performance evaluations
  • Continuous feedback encouraged between all team members
  • Performance metrics aligned with responsibilities outlined in the RACI matrix
  • Regular skills assessment and professional development planning

Future Growth and Scalability

  • Quarterly review and update of this organizational structure
  • Ongoing identification of potential new roles as the company expands
  • Implementation of cross-training initiatives to build versatility within the team
  • Investment in continued professional development opportunities for all team members
  • Regular reassessment of workflows and processes for optimization
  • Exploration of new markets and product lines for potential expansion
